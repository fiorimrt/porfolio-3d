Get to know me

Born in Argentina 36 years ago, Fiori started his career in web design as a self-taught designer at the age of 33. Driven by his passion for technology and digital art, he quickly acquired the skills to create websites. However, his curiosity led him to explore 3D design, a field where he found his true calling. Today, he owns a 3D design studio, working on projects across various industries such as architecture and advertising. With a creative vision and a commitment to continuous learning, Fiori has established himself as a key figure in his field.
